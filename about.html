Built on precision, run on experience.

Intac Industrial Systems is a Hyderabad-based manufacturer specialising in PP and FRP industrial equipment — designed, moulded and fabricated in-house.

Who we are

A reliable partner for corrosion-resistant equipment.

At Intac Industrial Systems, we specialise in the design and manufacturing of high-quality products crafted from Polypropylene (PP) and Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP). With a commitment to precision, sustainability, and client satisfaction, we stand as a reliable partner in delivering cutting-edge solutions for industrial and chemical process plants.

The company is led by proprietor K R Mohan Reddy and operates from our facility in Chengicherla village, Medchal-Malkajgiri District, Hyderabad.

What sets us apart

Backed by decades of expertise

Our specialists craft top-quality products that exceed expectations, covering a diverse range of PP/FRP fibreglass products and industrial process equipment.

Capability 01

Design & Fabrication

From innovative HDPE spiral tanks to efficient cooling towers, our commitment to excellence extends to the design and fabrication of industrial chemical process equipment.

Capability 02

Precast Moulding

Meticulous precast FRP mouldings for compound walls, kerbs and manholes, built for consistent quality across large civil-works quantities.

Capability 03

Protective Linings

Anticorrosive FRP linings and acid/alkali-resistant tile linings that extend the service life of process infrastructure.

Our process

Hand-laid, quality-checked, built to last.

Every vessel begins as a mould and is built up in layers of resin and reinforcement by our fabrication team — a process that rewards experience and close attention to detail at every stage, from lamination through curing and finishing.

Fabrication stages
  • 01 Requirement review & design against process conditions
  • 02 Mould preparation and material selection (PP / FRP / HDPE / MS)
  • 03 Hand lay-up, lamination and curing
  • 04 Finishing, quality check and dispatch
