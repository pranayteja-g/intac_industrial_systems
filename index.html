About the company

Precision fabrication, backed by decades of hands-on expertise.

At Intac Industrial Systems, we specialise in the design and manufacturing of high-quality products crafted from Polypropylene (PP) and Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP). Every tank, duct and mould leaving our Hyderabad facility is built around a simple commitment: precision, durability, and long-term client satisfaction.

Our specialists produce a diverse range of PP/FRP fibreglass products — from HDPE spiral tanks and cooling towers to precast FRP mouldings and full anticorrosive lining systems.

Materials & capability

Four materials. One fabrication standard.

Every project is engineered around the material best suited to the process conditions — chemical resistance, temperature, and structural load.

01 — PP

Polypropylene

Lightweight, chemically inert construction for tanks, ducting and process vessels handling aggressive media.

02 — FRP

Fiber Reinforced Plastic

High-strength, corrosion-resistant fabrication for storage tanks, scrubbers, reactors and moulded components.

03 — HDPE

High-Density Polyethylene

Spiral-wound tanks and scrubbers built for durability under continuous industrial duty cycles.

04 — MS

Mild Steel + FRP Lining

Structural steel vessels with FRP anticorrosive lining where mechanical strength and chemical resistance both matter.
