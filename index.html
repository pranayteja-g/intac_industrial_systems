About Us

At Intac Industrial Systems, we specialize in the design and manufacturing of high-quality products crafted from Polypropylene (PP) and Fiber Reinforced Plastic (FRP). With a commitment to precision, sustainability, and client satisfaction, we stand as a reliable partner in delivering cutting-edge solutions.

  • Backed by decades of expertise, our specialists craft top-quality products that exceed expectations. We specialize in producing a diverse range of PP/FRP fiberglass products.
  • From innovative HDPE spiral tanks to efficient cooling towers and meticulous precast FRP moldings, our commitment to excellence extends to the design and fabrication of industrial chemical process equipment.

Our Services

We manufacture:

  • PP/FRP
  • Fiber glass products
  • Design and fabrication of industrial chemical process equipment
  • HDPE spiral tanks
  • Cooling towers

Specialists in:

  • Precast FRP mouldings
  • Anticorrosive linings with FRP
